לשם יחוד קודשא בריך הוא ושכינתיה
For the sake of the unity of the Holy Blessed One and His Presence.
הריני מדפיס דברי תורה לשם מצווה
Behold, I publish these words of Torah for the sake of the mitzvah,
שיהיו לרבים ללמד, לדעת, ולעורר את הלב לאבינו שבשמים.
that they may help many to learn, to understand, and to awaken the heart toward our Father in Heaven.

This project creates a high‑quality, readable Hebrew Bible (Tanakh) e‑book, pairing clear Hebrew text with English and thoughtfully chosen artwork inspired by Marc Chagall.

The EPUB generator pulls text from Sefaria’s API, lays out chapters in a responsive, device‑friendly format, and places illustrations at meaningful chapters. A separate gallery showcases the artworks and their scriptural contexts.
